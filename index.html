About Us

Founded in 2025, AxisFlow Engineering offers tailored consulting services in compressed air systems, diesel and gasoline engines, battery systems, and turnkey project management. With a presence in Europe, India, and Africa, and multilingual expertise in Arabic, French, English, and German, we are committed to delivering efficiency and innovation to our clients.

Our Services

  • Compressed Air Systems
    Design, implementation, and optimization of compressed air systems (10–40 bar) with technical audits and compliance support.
  • Engine & Battery Design
    Expertise in designing and implementing diesel, gasoline engines, and battery systems for various industries.
  • Turnkey Project Management
    End-to-end management of industrial projects, including vendor evaluation and negotiation to reduce costs by up to 15%.

Our Projects

  • Sustainable Energy Plant
    A modernized facility with upgraded air, electrical, and piping systems to boost efficiency and support low-impact, renewable energy operations.
  • Production Line Relocation
    A full-scope turnkey project involving the dismantling, transport, and reinstallation of industrial equipment—ensuring minimal downtime, optimized layout, and improved operational efficiency in the new facility.
